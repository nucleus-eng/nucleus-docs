This is the release of v0.4.0, which includes stable Nucleus content (i.e., passing our internal testing and generally working).

Release: 23 July 2025

# General

- Nucleus is now Nucleus Distribution.
- The open science policy has been updated [Open science](https://www.notion.so/Open-science-1a1ae616eb51808ab804cc56fba9f514?pvs=21).
- A dedicated FAQs page has been added [FAQs](https://www.notion.so/FAQs-230ae616eb5180bdbd59fc8d018a7227?pvs=21).
- Developer Notes and [Nucleus Community Contributions](https://www.notion.so/Nucleus-Community-Contributions-0cc4d80c9448403f9f308b983999bd0e?pvs=21) is being sunset on Nucleus Distribution. Developer Notes will now appear on a dedicated site at https://devnotes.bnext.bio/.
- A section for tutorials has been introduced that includes [Nucleus Hub](https://www.notion.so/Nucleus-Hub-22dae616eb51802f9e99f51193a30a15?pvs=21), [Developer Notes](https://www.notion.so/Developer-Notes-22dae616eb5180bc9e76dc87f9af0e0e?pvs=21), and [Making Synthetic Cells for Beginners](https://www.notion.so/Making-Synthetic-Cells-for-Beginners-cbafa6d239714913a3186cd07a9d01aa?pvs=21).
- The Development section is being renamed Software.

# Synthetic Cells

- The ‚ÄúHello, World‚Äù Liposome Protocol has been renamed [PURE Cell](https://www.notion.so/PURE-Cell-412dfbe9ffd941bfab16b69ec866de27?pvs=21) and is now categorized as a Synthetic Cell.
- The protocol ‚ÄúBooting up a simple synthetic cell‚Äù has been renamed [Making Synthetic Cells for Beginners](https://www.notion.so/Making-Synthetic-Cells-for-Beginners-cbafa6d239714913a3186cd07a9d01aa?pvs=21) and placed under Guides.

# Content

- The [OnePot PURE workshop module](https://www.notion.so/OnePot-PURE-workshop-module-238ae616eb5180fa8e10f1accf3c7cf3?pvs=21) includes new DNA content encoding for the 36 PURE proteins in the pET28a backbone.

# Cytosol

- [Make OnePot Protein Mix](https://www.notion.so/Make-OnePot-Protein-Mix-234ae616eb5180c0b84cf5046d63a803?pvs=21) is added as an alternative to the existing 36-pot protocol for making PURE protein mix.
- [Make Ribosomes](https://www.notion.so/Make-Ribosomes-1e5ae616eb518007a24be26793ffc152?pvs=21) introduces a protocol for purifying *E. coli* ribosomes from A19 biomass.
- [Make tRNAs](https://www.notion.so/Make-tRNAs-1a0ae616eb51806b9976e46979ed4b94?pvs=21) is updated to recommend purifying from A19 biomass over MRE600.
- The structure of [PURE Protocols](https://www.notion.so/PURE-Protocols-0cd5d3af44aa4d5e85871a75a7c9161b?pvs=21) has been re-arranged for clarity.
