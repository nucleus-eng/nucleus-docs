# Magnitude–Unit Spacing Test

## Should fire (missing space between magnitude and unit)

Spin the lysate at 5000g for 15 min.
Pellet precipitated proteins at 14 000g.
Begin the starter culture at 6pm.
Begin the run at 12pm.
Elute with 500mL of buffer.
Filter through a 0.22µm membrane.
Dilute the stock to 50mM before use.
Incubate at 37°C for 30s on ice.
Run the SDS-PAGE at 200V for 1h.
Load 10µL of each sample.
Add 100µg of lysozyme to the lysis buffer.
The protein runs at approximately 25kDa.
Centrifuge at 10000rcf for 10 min.
Measure absorbance at 660nm.
Use a 30mm filter membrane.

## Should NOT fire (correct spacing, or non-targets)

Spin the lysate at 5000 g for 15 min.
Pellet precipitated proteins at 14 000 g.
Begin the starter culture at 6 pm.
Begin the run at 12 pm.
Add 50 mg of lysozyme.
Dilute to 40 µg per reaction.
Resuspend 3.6 gDCM of biomass.
Use a 0.5 kg counterweight.
Elute with 500 mL of buffer.
Filter through a 0.22 µm membrane.
Dilute the stock to 50 mM before use.
Incubate at 37 °C for 30 s on ice.
Run the SDS-PAGE at 200 V for 1 h.
Load 10 µL of each sample.
The protein runs at approximately 25 kDa.
Centrifuge at 10 000 rcf for 10 min.
Measure absorbance at 660 nm.
Use the 10x buffer concentrate.
Perform 3 wash steps.
