# Space Before Degree Test

## Should fire (space before the degree symbol)

Store the pellets at 4 °C until use.
Keep the stock at −80 °C for long-term storage.

## Should NOT fire (correct — no space)

Store the pellets at 4°C until use.
Keep the stock at −80°C for long-term storage.
Incubate at 37°C / 260 rpm / 16 hr.
